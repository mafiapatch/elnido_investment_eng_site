About Us

About US


H Group Properties is the real estate arm of H Hospitality Group, a brand distinguished for setting new standards in luxury island hospitality. Expanding into real estate, the group applies its expertise in service and design to create investment-worthy residences and condotels. Committed to long-term value, we develop landmark properties that elevate island life and drive regional growth.
Guided by this principle, we shape El Nido’s future with developments that embody sustainability and refined living. Rooted in respect for nature, our projects blend contemporary elegance with a dedication to enriching the local community through eco-conscious practices and meaningful opportunities.
More than properties, we create lasting legacies through thoughtful innovation and steadfast purpose in one of the world’s most breathtaking destinations.
