About H Properties

H Properties specializes in high-end resort investments in El Nido, featuring two flagship projects—Mu Nacpan Beach and Piece Lio. Mu Nacpan Beach is El Nido’s premier oceanfront resort investment, offering exceptional planning, design, and comprehensive resort facilities for an unparalleled luxury experience. Piece Lio, a collaboration between H Properties and the renowned Japanese brand Piece, is the fastest-growing hotel project in El Nido. It combines international brand excellence with the region’s natural beauty to create a unique stay for travelers. H Properties continues to develop premium resort destinations, integrating outstanding design and top-tier services to deliver exceptional value for both investors and guests.
