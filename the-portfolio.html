Associated Brands


With a strong foundation in hospitality, we have developed and operated eight hotels across El Nido, spanning from mid-range to luxury island hospitality. Building on this expertise, H Hospitality Group is expanding into high-value resort investments, condotels, and residential developments—offering both exceptional stays and ownership opportunities through its real estate arm, H Group Properties.
Our portfolio includes two flagship projects—Piece Lio and Mu Nacpan Beach. Piece Lio, developed in collaboration with Japan’s renowned Piece brand, is one of El Nido’s fastest-growing hotel projects, combining international hospitality excellence with the region’s natural beauty to offer a distinct travel experience. Mu Nacpan Beach, soon to launch, will set a new benchmark for oceanfront resort investment in El Nido, featuring world-class planning, architecture, and resort facilities.
As we continue to grow, we look forward to introducing more transformative developments that bridge the gap between hospitality and real estate investment while providing lasting value to investors, guests, and the local community.
