Signature Island Adventures

Cruise, paddle, and dive through El Nido’s marine treasures.

  • Island Hopping in Bacuit BayBay – Classic tours A to D with iconic limestone cliffs,lagoons, coves, beaches, and kayak spots with the most iconic spots:
    • Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Cadlao Lagoon, Shimizu Island, Secret Beach
  • Scuba diving, freediving, and reef snorkeling – Explore El Nido’s marine sanctuaries below the surface
  • Private boat charters, sunset sails – Bespoke cruising for special moments
  • Extended Island Expeditions – Immersive journeys to remote islands, hidden beaches, and off-grid communities

Coastal Beaches & Hideaways

From surf breaks to secret coves, El Nido’s coastlines offer escapes for all

  • Lio Beach – Walkable beachfront with sunbeds, cafes, and nature paths
  • Nacpan Beach – 4km stretch of golden sand with laid-back beach bars
  • Vanilla Beach – Accessible beachfront mall with restaurants, shops, and events
  • Duli Beach – Waves perfect for surfing and watching the sun dip low
  • Sibaltan Beach – East-facing, local boats, and seasonal manta ray sightings
  • Teneguiban Pier – Secluded coastline with flat-water kitesurfing, jump-off to Daracoton and Linapacan
  • Daracoton Island – Quiet and off-grid; great for a peaceful island day
  • Linapacan Island – Untouched islands with crystal-clear waters

Nature Trails & Lookouts

Hikes, heights, and hidden forest wonders for those seeking perspective or a refreshing plunge.

  • Canopy Walk – A guided trek above El Nido town with panoramic bay views
  • Nagkalit-kalit Falls – Light jungle hike to a refreshing forest waterfall
  • Bulalacao Falls – Remote twin falls
  • Mt. Mansilawit – One of El Nido’s highest peaks for seasoned hikers
  • Ille Cave – Archaeological cave system with limestone climbs and historical finds

The Lio Lifestyle

Ayala Land’s 325-hectare master-planned lifestyle estate in El Nido, with nature trails,residential enclaves, beachfront access, and airport connectivity

  • Lio Beach – Walkable beachfront with sunbeds, cafes, and nature paths
  • Lio Airport – Direct air gateway to El Nido, seamlessly connected to the estate
  • Kalye Artisano – A creative hub for boutique shopping, cafes, hands-on cooking classes, and artisan workshops
  • The Earth School – A sustainability-focused international primary school
  • Mangrove Kayaking – Inland paddling trails that weave through the estate’s protected wetlands
  • Lakwatsero Motorcycles & Adventure Tours – Guided dirt bike tours through trails that explore El Nido and beyond
  • Yoga Tayo – Daily yoga and mat pilates held in open-air studios

Culinary & Wellness

Experiences that celebrate good food, good flow, and good living.

  • Verde – Upscale Plant-based elevated dining
  • Piece of Sky – Sunset-view rooftop bar with elevated Filipino comfort food
  • Sunmai – Beachfront bar with casual eats, music, and cocktails
  • Grounded – Artisan coffee shop known for its calm atmosphere and curated brews
  • Oriental Spa – Wellness haven offering massages and holistic treatments
