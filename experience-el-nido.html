Activities in El Nido

Island hopping, in-land tours

Island Hopping

El Nido is famous for its breathtaking island-hopping tours, offering visitors the chance to explore crystal-clear lagoons, pristine white-sand beaches, and stunning limestone cliffs. Popular stops include Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Secret Beach, and Snake Island, each showcasing the region’s natural beauty. Whether kayaking through emerald waters or snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs, island hopping in El Nido is an unforgettable experience.

In-Land Tours

Beyond the islands, El Nido’s inland adventures offer a different kind of exploration. Visitors can trek to Taraw Cliff for a panoramic view of Bacuit Bay, relax at Nacpan Beach’s golden shores, or explore waterfalls and caves hidden within lush jungles. ATV rides and motorbike tours provide an exciting way to discover the area’s scenic landscapes, making in-land tours an essential part of the El Nido experience.
