Sustainability

Social Support

H Hospitality Group employs 300 staff members and is the largest hospitality group in El Nido. We take pride in contributing to the livelihoods of approximately 300 families. Over 70% of our employees are local residents, reflecting our commitment to supporting the community by providing decent jobs and competitive wages. We believe that stable employment and a nurturing work environment are essential for building a thriving community. Our employees are the foundation of our success, and their well-being remains our top priority.

Sea Turtle Conservation

We take pride in our sea turtle conservation efforts, regularly conducting training sessions for our employees to emphasize the importance of protecting these creatures and extending this education to our guests. Each year, we release between 8,000 and 10,000 sea turtles back into the ocean. As of 2024, we have successfully rescued and rehabilitated over 49,500 sea turtles, making a significant contribution to marine ecosystem preservation.

Brigada Eskwela Program

We recognize the importance of education in building a brighter future for our community. H Hospitality Group actively participates in the Brigada Eskwela program, donating essential materials such as cement, hollow blocks, and paint, while also providing manpower to assist in cleaning, painting, and repairing school facilities. This year’s beneficiaries include Bucana National High School and Calitang Elementary School. Additionally, we plan to adopt a school to ensure sustainability and continuous development.

H Hospitality Group also supports various schools by offering on-the-job training opportunities for students. Interns are assigned to different HHG properties, including roles in food and beverage, housekeeping, and front desk operations, as well as corporate departments such as human resources, accounting, and procurement.

Pursuing Excellence Through Community

H Hospitality Group supports local sports initiatives to foster teamwork, discipline, and community spirit. Our basketball team is a testament to our commitment to these values, achieving a significant milestone in 2023 by winning the championship. This victory not only brought pride to our organization but also inspired our community to strive for excellence both on and off the court.

This year, HHG will establish its own choir to showcase vocal talent and a dance club to highlight creative expression through movement. These initiatives demonstrate that our excellence extends beyond the hospitality industry, as we nurture diverse talents. By providing opportunities for artistic expression and personal growth, they promote work-life balance within our community. Additionally, these clubs aim to engage with and give back to the community through performances and events, bringing people together to celebrate local talent.

HHG Choir: The Sound of El Nido

The HHG Choir is an internal employee group within H Hospitality Group Corporation (HHG), united by a shared love for music. As the first hospitality choir in El Nido, it embodies the spirit of HHG employees, bringing together members from all our locations—Town, Lio, and Nacpan—to create harmonious melodies.

The choir made its debut on August 18, 2024, at the hotel basketball friendly match, opening the event with a heartfelt rendition of the national anthem. This performance brought pride to HHG and joy to everyone in attendance. Composed of 13 employees from various HHG departments, the choir introduced the sound of music to El Nido.

More than just a choir, the HHG Choir is a testament to our commitment to work-life balance and employee engagement. Through this initiative, our employees have found new ways to express themselves, connect with colleagues, and bring their passions into the workplace. With the support of the HR department, the choir reinforces our belief that HHG is more than just a workplace—it is a vibrant community where everyone has a voice. As the choir continues to grow, they are already preparing for upcoming performances, further strengthening their connection with the greater El Nido community.

Animal Neutering Initiative

We collaborate with the renowned Vet in Practice animal hospital in Manila to host free spay and neuter programs for stray dogs and cats every week. Over the course of a month, we accommodate six groups of veterinarians from Manila, with five vets per group. These initiatives effectively control the stray animal population, improve their living conditions, and enhance the overall community environment.

Nutritional-Lunch-program

Recognizing the challenge of malnutrition among some elementary school students, we have launched a nutritious lunch program to provide them with balanced meals. This initiative ensures that local children receive the essential nutrients needed for growth and learning, laying a stronger foundation for their future.

Christmas Gift Giving Event

Every Christmas, we bring joy to local children by giving out gifts. This tradition not only strengthens our bond with the community but also adds warmth and smiles to countless children's holidays.
