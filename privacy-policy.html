Privacy Policy


At PIECES, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

may collect the following types of information :
Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you fill out forms, request downloads, or subscribe to our updates.
Usage Data: Information on how you use our site, such as pages visited, time spent, and device type (collected via cookies or analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to :
Provide information about our properties and services
Send you newsletters, updates, or promotional offers (if you’ve opted in)
Improve website functionality and user experience
Respond to your inquiries or requests

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share it with trusted service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., email platforms, analytics services) under confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

6. Your Rights

You may request to :
Access the personal data we hold about you
Correct or delete your information
Unsubscribe from communications at any time
