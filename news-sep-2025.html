H Hospitality Group Achieves Dual Success: Local Impact and Global Recognition

H Hospitality Group (HHG) has recently made significant strides on both the local and international fronts, demonstrating its core values of social responsibility and world-class excellence.


On the local stage, HHG successfully hosted its second annual blood donation drive at the Barangay Maligaya Multipurpose Hall in El Nido, Palawan. Partnering with the Philippine Red Cross - Palawan Chapter, the event collected nearly 50 bags of blood, providing a much-needed boost to local medical resources and offering tangible help to patients in need. The enthusiastic participation from both HHG employees and local volunteers underscored the company's commitment to "corporate growth and social responsibility go hand in hand."

Meanwhile, HHG's dedication to quality has been recognized internationally. The company's Piece Lio hotel was honored with a spot on the prestigious Michelin Guide’s hotel recommendations list. This is a remarkable achievement, as only 20 of the 18,800 hotels in the Philippines were selected. Piece Lio now stands alongside numerous five-star establishments, becoming a point of pride for El Nido and a new focus on the global tourism stage.


The Michelin Guide praised the hotel's unique blend of Japanese architectural style with the natural charm of Palawan. The review highlighted the collaboration between H Hotel Group and the Piece brand, noting the "fresh, minimalist aesthetic" and "consistent design philosophy" evident throughout the property. This recognition confirms that Piece Lio's design, service, and architecture meet world-class standards and demonstrates HHG's competitive position in the global market.


By simultaneously improving community life and earning international acclaim, HHG has proven its ability to lead with both purpose and quality.
